Our Story

Three Decades of
Culinary Excellence

Founded in 1987 by Chef Antonio Rossi in the heart of Tuscany, La Pasta Bella began as a small family trattoria with a dream: to share authentic Italian flavors with the world.

Today, our Mumbai outpost under Executive Chef Maria Bellini brings that legacy to India with a unique Indo-Italian fusion. We use locally sourced paneer, Indian spices, and farm-fresh vegetables alongside imported Italian cheeses and olive oils — creating dishes that feel both familiar and exciting to the Indian palate.
