Our Story

Three Decades of Excellence

Founded in 1987 by Chef Antonio Rossi in Tuscany, La Pasta Bella shares authentic Italian flavors. Today, our Mumbai outpost brings Indo-Italian fusion using local paneer, Indian spices, and imported Italian cheeses — creating dishes for the Indian palate.
